Downloading

You can get my pre-compiled eAccelerator from here.

Installing

Drop the downloaded file under C:\XAMPP\php\ext\ and replace the existing one

Go back to your php.ini, search for eAccelerator and uncomment this line:


zend_extension = "C:\xampp\php\ext\eaccelerator.dll"


eAccelerator is now installed!

Now with all of this done, head to Downloading ScratchR
